  • WHAT IS EZTHROWS?

  • EZ means easy, so it's like easy to throw. EZTHROWS is a hub for everything
    throws coaching, training, and education. Run by EZTAB owner Angelo Lebaste
    and former shotput thrower in University of Baguio.

  • How did we get started?

  • After having success developing local
    high school throwers into Benguet and all
    throwers, and growing a training group
    of post-collegiate national-class athletes,
    we set out to make the training programs
    and coaching practices that developed
    our elite throwers accessible to anyone.
    We found that many coaches across the
    Philippines hold too tightly to their
    knowledge of the throws. We disagree
    with keeping training "secrets" and thus
    ne of our primary goals is to make
    high-level throws training and education
    available to anyone who wants it.
